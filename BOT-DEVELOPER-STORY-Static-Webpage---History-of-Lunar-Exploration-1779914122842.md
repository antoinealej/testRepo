# Static Webpage — History of Lunar Exploration

**Branch:** main

## Description
As a visitor, I want to access a static webpage presenting the history of lunar exploration so that I can discover the major missions, milestones, and technological achievements related to the exploration of the Moon in a clear and engaging format.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The webpage shall be fully static and require no backend functionality or user authentication.
- The webpage shall include an introduction section about the history of lunar exploration.
- The webpage shall display a chronological timeline of major lunar exploration missions.
- The webpage shall include key milestones such as Apollo 11, robotic lunar missions, and modern lunar programs.
- The webpage shall contain images or illustrations related to lunar exploration.
- The webpage shall include a section describing future lunar exploration initiatives.
- The webpage shall be responsive and usable on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- The webpage shall provide clear navigation with distinct sections and headings.
- The webpage shall use accessible typography and sufficient color contrast.
- All images on the webpage shall include descriptive alt text for accessibility compliance.
- The webpage shall render correctly on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
- The webpage content shall be written in grammatically correct English.
- The webpage shall optimize performance through compressed images and minimal unnecessary scripts.
- The webpage shall maintain a reasonable initial page load time.
